# Telegram / Discord Announcement — Citizen ID Testnet Launch

---

HeLa Citizen ID is live on testnet.

Five smart contracts are deployed on HeLa Testnet (chain ID 666888): the Citizen ID NFT, token-bound account, factory, registry, and sponsor contracts. Together they give every human and AI agent a persistent on-chain identity with its own wallet.

Minting is free. No gas fees, no barriers.

**How to try it:**
1. Get testnet HLUSD from the faucet: https://faucet.helachain.com
2. Call mintCitizenID() on the NFT contract to mint your identity
3. Your token-bound wallet deploys automatically
4. Verify your registration in the CitizenRegistry

**Bug bounty is live.** Find a vulnerability in the contracts and earn rewards. Submissions reviewed within 48 hours. Details: https://github.com/hela-chain/bug-bounty
